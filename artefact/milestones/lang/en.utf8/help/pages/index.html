Milestones

Milestones are simple ToDo lists. They are particularly well-suited to milestone medium and long-term endeavours. A milestone can consist of a number of individual activities with which completion dates are associated.

You could use the functionality to:

  • set yourself individual goals for a large fact.
  • manage your personal learning activities and keep an eye on how many you have already accomplished during the time frame that you have set yourself.
  • keep track of workshops you have attended for your professional development over time.
